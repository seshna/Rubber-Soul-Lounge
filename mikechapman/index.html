About

I have a Bachelor of Music Composition and Production
from Australian Institute of Music (AIM) Sydney

A musician, producer, and composer from Greater Western Sydney who now resides in Perth.

I am the sound engineer of the Rubber Soul Lounge, responsible for mixing and recording artists. Also a teacher at Penny Lanes Music School, I give workshops on home studio recording and music composition.

Proficient in the use of Digital Audio Workstations such as Logic, Pro Tools, Reason, and Ableton. If you would like to learn how to make your own quality demos at home or even if you want to learn basic production techniques and ways to improve your songwriting, get in touch.

Services

I offer production workshops and recording sessions.

Sound Production

Proficient in the use of Digital Audio Workstations such as Logic, Pro Tools, Reason, and Ableton. If you would like to learn how to make your own quality demos at home or even if you want to learn basic production techniques and ways to improve your songwriting, get in touch.

You have 2 options for payment: $50 per hour session, or $40 per hour if you book 4 classes.

Recording Session

$120 for a bootleg session of 5 songs at around 5 minutes each.
Spend your first hour setting up and rehearsing, the second hour I'll record you, the final hour will be doing a live mix down and CD burning

Additional Options

1 Additional hour = $50. (Includes my time & Room hire)
**Note if a band is booked to rehearse in the next session you'll need to wrap it up.

I do not offer you each individual recorded instrument track
To have me take your recording home and mix and master it for you = $150 (1 hour for each song for 5 songs).
